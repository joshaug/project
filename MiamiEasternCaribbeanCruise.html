Sailing onboard Lady of the Sea

A cruise on the Lady of the Sea allows you to escape all the stresses and worries of normal life and enjoy a holiday that will relax, rejuvenate and entertain you. Once onboard you can head for the Spa and enjoy some invigorating treatments. Opt for a massage or oxygen facial, visit the Thermal Suite or head for the Salt Room that mimics the salt caves of Eastern Europe and is guaranteed to soothe mind and body. Enjoy some exercise in the fresh ocean air at the three-storey sports complex. Hang out by the pool deck and pool bar.

Day 1 - Miami, Florida

Miami was developed by a railroad magnate and made famous by superstars who thrive on its churning nightlife and live in its lavish waterfront estates.

Days 2 and 3 - At sea

Day 4 - St. Thomas, American Virgin Islands

Shopping is one of the big onshore attractions in Charlotte Amalie, the capital of the US Virgin Islands. Off shore, colourful coral reefs in turquoise waters lure snorkelers and scuba divers from all over the world.

Day 5 - Road Town, Tortola, British Virgin Islands

Superb white sand beaches, big surf, visiting yachts and a collection of lively beach bars make Tortola a popular stop.

Day 6 - At sea

Day 7 - Nassau, Bahamas

While you're here, try your bargaining skills at the Straw Market or choose a historic tour, ride a submarine, sail a catamaran, pet a dolphin or try your luck in a world-class casino.
